Who We Are

Study Group is a platform specially created for diploma students. We started it in 2023 to provide students with quality study materials and a collaborative learning environment. Our goal is to help every student succeed.

Meet Our Team Join Community

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to provide quality study materials like notes and books to diploma students for free, so that no student is left behind due to lack of resources.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to make quality education accessible to every diploma student in India, enabling them to succeed in their careers and contribute to the country's development.

Our Journey

2023 - The Beginning

Started Study Group with a vision to help diploma students access quality study materials for free.

2023 - First 1000 Users

Reached our first milestone of 1000 active users and uploaded 100+ study materials.

2024 - Community Growth

Expanded to 2000+ users and launched WhatsApp and Telegram communities for better student interaction.

2024 - 5000+ Users

Achieved 5000+ active users and 500+ study materials, becoming the largest free study resource platform for diploma students.

2025- 2026 - Future Goals

Planning to expand to more states, add video tutorials, and create a mobile app for better accessibility.
